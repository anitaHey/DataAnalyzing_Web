Intro

We've pre-selected several academic degrees which are most likely to appear in a job description of a software engineering job position, including PhD, Master, MBA, BA, BS, and Bachelor. Then we counted the frequencies of these keywords in all job descriptions and visualized them via the following charts.

Summary

  • Google: Most jobs require at least a Bachelor degree, but further education (e.g., Master and PhD) is preferred.
  • Apple: Most jobs mention "BS" in job descriptions.
  • Facebook: Lots of jobs doesn't even mention a degree requirement.
